The Development of Bigbang in ten years

G-Dragon Team MT

  • In 2009, BIGBANG went to Japan to develop and debuted in Japan with the song "My Heaven"
  • On July 24, 2010, BIGBANG participated in the "Asia-Pacific Global Tolerance with Music2010" event held in Japan and won the Global Unity Award from the United Nations.
  • In 2011, BIGBANG returned to the Korean music scene as a combination and released the fourth mini album "Big Bang Mini Album Vol. 4", becoming the first Asian group to win the "Worldwide Act" MTV European Music Awards in history, and in 2012 In the first half of the year, he went to the official website of the American Grammy and became the first Korean singer to be introduced by Grammy.
  • In February 2012, BIGBANG released the mini 5th album "ALIVE", which included "Blue", "Bad Boy", "Love Dust", "Fantastic Baby" and other songs; in July, BIGBANG toured China for the first time.
  • In 2013, the five members each performed solo music activities. In April 2014, 18 artists such as BIGBANG became shareholders of the company YG Entertainment.
  • On April 1, 2015, BIGBANG decided to return, and released the "MADE" series of digital single albums in May, June, July, August, and September, respectively. It was also BIGBANG's group return after three years; "MADE "The total sales of four MINI albums exceeded 3.4 million.
  • On August 20, 2016, BIGBANG held a concert to commemorate the tenth anniversary of its debut in Seoul, South Korea. During the nearly three-hour performance that night, BIGBANG performed a total of 19 works, and spent a wonderful and memorable night with fans.
  • On December 8, 2016, BIGBANG's regular digital album "MADE FULL ALBUM" was sold on QQ Music and Kugou. On December 12, the audio was officially launched, and the first new song "FXXK IT" was released. It was jointly produced by GD and YG music producer TEDDY Make.
  • In January 2018, won the 32nd Korea Golden Record Award in the audio source department.
  • In March 2019, group member SEUNGRI terminated the contract with YG due to a series of scandals and announced their withdrawal from the entertainment industry.
